Arcane Archer

"Hey, I'm Dylan, and I'm excited to share how 'Arcane Archer' came to life. It's one of my early attempts at making a bigger game, although I already had some experience in game development. Using Unity and C#, I jumped into creating 'Arcane Archer' with enthusiasm. But it wasn't all smooth sailing. I faced challenges like figuring out how to make the game fun to play and balancing the difficulty levels.

While I used some pre-made graphics and code snippets from the internet to speed things up, a lot of the game's creation was down to my own hard work and creativity. Throughout the process, I asked for feedback from friends and other developers, which helped me improve the game and learn a ton along the way.

Finishing 'Arcane Archer' filled me with pride. It showed me how much I've learned and how far I've come in game development. Even though 'Arcane Archer' is one of my early projects, it's just the beginning. I'm excited to keep learning and making even cooler games in the future. Thanks for being a part of this journey with me!"

The Lost Runner

Introducing "The Lost Runner," an addictive endless runner adventure for your mobile device. And here's the twist—every bit of this thrilling journey, from the cool graphics to the exciting gameplay, was all made by me! In "The Lost Runner," you'll dash through a mysterious world I've created, jumping and crouching to avoid obstacles as you try to beat your highest score.

Using Unity, I've made sure the game runs smoothly on your phone, so you can play anytime, anywhere. So, get ready to embark on a fun-filled adventure in "The Lost Runner," where every jump and slide was crafted with care by me!

Finance app design

Introducing my latest mobile finance app! It's crafted to help you manage your money smoothly and stylishly. I've chosen a clean design with a white background, accented by a light purple box for your balance, light lavender buttons, and midnight black for important text.
This app is all about simplicity and ease of use. The light purple box adds a touch of refinement to display your balance, while the light lavender buttons make navigation a breeze. Meanwhile, the midnight black text ensures that important information stands out clearly.
Navigating the app is straightforward. Whether you're checking your balance or setting a budget, every screen is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Javascript Webshop

This JavaScript Webshop, developed as a school project, focuses on functionality over design. I fully focused on the Javascript and did not style it. Features include adding items to the cart through a modal, viewing cart contents, a purchasing option, and tooltips for user experience.
